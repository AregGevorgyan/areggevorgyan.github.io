A really fast simulation of Conway's Game of Life in the browser.

This is an interactive implementation of Conway's Game of Life in a browser <canvas> element, which can simulate as many as 2100 cells over 250 generations in just milliseconds. The secret is Bill Gosper's wonderful Hashlife algorithm, which combines quadtrees and memoization in a really nice way to "compress space and time". In the image, you can see the algorithm computing the 498,913,509,376th generation of a Turing machine pattern in Conway's Game of Life, in under 1 millisecond!
